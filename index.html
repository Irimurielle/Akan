The Akan name giver

Welcome, this web application uses birth date to calculate the day of the week one was born and then basing on the gender,finds the Akan name of the person.
Akan names are derived from Ghanian culture. Frequently in Ghana, children are given their first name as a 'day name' which corresponds to the day in the week they were born. Learn more
